Future Work

A possibility for what could be done in future predictions using the Yelp Dataset is to create predictions based off of different groupings or clusterings. This gives a more broad prediction and leaves room for improvement. Even though being able to predict a possible group or family of diseases would not be as great as predicting a specific disease, it would still be a good feat to be able to use a social media outlet to predict diseases.
